Recipe for pasta dish with tomato and mozzarella

IngrediÃ«nts
- cherrytomatoes
- 2 small or one big white onion
- 1 zucchini
- 250g fusili (or any pasta you like)
- 300g of minced beef or any meat substitute
- 250ml milk
- 500ml water (250ml if you are using canned tomatosauce)
- mozzarella
- mix for tomato sauce, or canned tomato sauce
- 1 clove of garlic

Directions
- Preheat the oven at 200 degrees Celsius
- Cut all vegetables
- Cook your meat or meat substitue
- Add your zucchini and let it cook for 5 minutes, until softened
- Add the tomatoes and onion and let cook for a few minutes
- Add the garlic and let it cook for a few more minutes
- Add the (mix for) tomatosauce, the milk and water and let it simmer for a few minutes
- Meanwhile, put your uncooked pasta in an oven dish
- Put the sauce over the pasta, it should be fully covered
- Put the dish in the oven for 30 minutes
- While in the oven, slice your mozzarella in small pieces
- After the 30 minutes, put the mozzarella on top of the dish
- Let it broil for 5 to 10 minutes
- Enjoy!
