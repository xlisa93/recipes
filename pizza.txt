Here I write the recipe for my favorite pizza.

Ingredients
- pizza dough (fresh or pre-made)
- sauce (tomato or creme fraishe)
- mozzarella
- grated parmesan cheese
- goat cheese
- gorgonzola

Directions
- preheat the oven according to the recipe directions
- top your pizza with the sauce, then add all cheeses
- put it in the oven for about 10 minutes (time depends on oven)
- Enjoy!
